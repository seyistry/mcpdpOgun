TO ALL THE PARTICIPANTS WHO HAVE REGISTERED FOR THE SELECTED EDITION OF MCPDP OGUN STATE COMMENCING ON 27TH JANUARY 2020

  • Attendance is compulsory and 100%. Every participants shall sign-in and out on every day of the programme.
  • All participants should ensure adequate and maximum release from their respective health institution to attend the programme for the required number of days. Not all excuses will be tolerated please.

Registration Procedure

  •      Pay the stipulated fee to Guaranty Trust Bank Plc with the following details:
                     ACCOUNT NAME : MCPDPOG
                     ACCOUNT NUMBER : 0052781970 (USE THIS ACCOUNT NUMBER ONLY)
                     AMOUNT : N20,000 (Normal).
  •      All funds are Non-Refundable (Clarify Edition).
  •      Use the name on your licence for payment at the bank.
  •      Register with the name on your practising license only.
  •      TELLER IS VALID FOR 2 MONTHS FROM DATE OF PAYMENT.
  •      Use your teller number to complete your registration.
  •      Please register with only one teller which is N20,000 only.
  •      Attend the Selected programme and Date otherwise money will be forfeited.
  •      Register only once per programme.
  •      Obtain a print out.
  •      Come to the venue with your completed registration form printout, practising license and bank teller.
